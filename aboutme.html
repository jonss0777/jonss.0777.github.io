About Me

Hi! I am Jonathan a senior from Queens College pursuing a Computer BS Degree. During the past 8 moths I have interned at the Advance Science Research Center where I have collaborated in the design of a city wide DIY air quality sensor initative.
This initiative aims to teach people how to create their own air quality sensors.

We have(and sometimes host workshops for the local community) a detailed guide in our github on how to set up[soldering and wiring] the sensors to send the sensor data to a google sheets[programming and client server model] using Google App Script. Once enough data is collected individuals can analyze and share their findings.
